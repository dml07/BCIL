MSc/PhD Position in Optics/Photonics

We are seeking multiple enthusiastic MSc and PhD students to work on experimental and algorithmic developments for novel coherent optical imaging systems.

Research Areas

  • Applied Optics
  • Optical Microscopy
  • Fourier Optics
  • Coherent Imaging
  • Optical Superresolution

Required Skills

  • A BSc or MSc degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Physics, or any relevant field.
  • A good understanding of fundamentals of optics and signal processing.
  • Programming experience in LabView, Matlab, or Python.
  • Good communication skills both in written and spoken English.

Funding & Benefits

  • Fully funded including tuiton waiver and monthly stipend. (Stipend will follow TÜBİTAK rates.)
  • Additional benefits from IMU includes meal allowances and discounted on campus housing.
