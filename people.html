Tolga Gürcan

Graduate Research Assistant/Year(2020-2022)

Current Research Areas

Ph.D. , Electrical and Computer Engineering (Princeton University, Princeton/NJ/USA)

Projects

  • Biophotonics and Optical Imaging Laboratory (September 2020 – June 2022): Working on off-axis digital holography for phase imaging, total internal reflection microscopy, UV grayscale lithography under the direction of Assist. Prof. Muhammed Fatih Toy.
  • Medical & Biological Physics Research Group (May 2017 – June 2022): Working on ultrafast laser development, photoacoustic imaging, holographic image projection with spatial light modulator, total internal reflection digital holographic microscopy under the direction of Prof. Burçin Ünlü.
  • Lumos Laser (July 2018 - September 2018): Worked on electronic control of an ultrashort pulsed laser such as creating trigger signal for AOM by using an input signal from oscillator’s photodiode and controlling different stage’s power by controlling corresponding diode drivers from FPGA.
  • EMT Control (June 2019 - July 2019): Worked on FPGA based DC motor control systems
